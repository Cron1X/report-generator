Encounter 31 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on quantities, shapes, ordering adjectives, giving advice, and food-related language. The aim was to describe objects clearly and give simple advice.

What You Practised

You worked with quantity expressions.
Examples you practised:

a few tables
lots of pasta
a piece of cake
some vegetables

You practised ordering adjectives.
Examples:

the large red apple
the small green cake

You worked with advice language using should and shouldn’t.
Examples:

You should eat lots of fruit.
You shouldn’t eat a lot of cake.

You practised talking about prices and quantities.
Examples:

$5 per piece
$2 each

You also practised giving health advice.
Examples:

You should eat more vegetables.
You mustn’t drink too many soft drinks.
