Encounter 21 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on talking about experiences and giving opinions.

What You Practised

You practised talking about experiences.
Examples:

I’ve been to London.
I’ve tried sushi.

You practised giving opinions.
Examples:

I liked the film.
It was interesting.
I didn’t enjoy the book.

You practised explaining opinions.
Examples:

I liked it because it was funny.
I didn’t like it because it was boring.

What to Practise More

1. Giving Reasons

I liked the film because it was interesting.

2. Using Full Sentences

I didn’t enjoy the book because it was difficult.
