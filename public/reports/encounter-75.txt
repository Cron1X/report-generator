Encounter 75 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on entertainment, television, idioms, and the present perfect continuous. The unit developed conversational fluency and descriptive language.

What You Practised

You worked on entertainment vocabulary.
Examples practised:

reality shows
reruns
on-demand programming
cable TV
streaming

You practised talking about viewing habits.
Examples:

I watch most shows on TV.
I watch shows on my laptop.

You worked on idiomatic expressions.
Examples:

kill two birds with one stone
let the cat out of the bag
hit the nail on the head
cost an arm and a leg

You practised the present perfect and present perfect continuous.
Examples:

I have practiced my listening skills by watching TV.
I have been practicing my listening skills by watching TV.

You practised asking about experiences.
Examples:

Have you ever seen this show?
How long have you watched it?

You practised summarizing stories.
Examples:

In a nutshell…
To make a long story short…

What to Practise More

Idiom Usage
Practise using idioms in full sentences.

Tense Choice
Focus on the difference between have done and have been doing.

Final Comment

This unit focused on entertainment and fluency. To improve further, practise combining idioms with accurate tense usage.
