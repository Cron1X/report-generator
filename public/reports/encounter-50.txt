Encounter 50 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on money, the economy, paying methods, permission and obligation, and linking contrasting ideas. The unit also developed decision-making and discussion skills.

What You Practised

You worked on economic vocabulary.
Examples you practised:

recession
inflation
interest rate
credit
investment

The economy is booming.
There is high unemployment.

You practised ways of paying.
Examples:

pay by credit card
pay by cash
bank transfer

You worked with either…or.
Examples:

You can either pay by cash or credit card.

You practised let and make.
Examples:

The store doesn’t let you pay by card.
The store makes you pay in cash.

You practised linking ideas with contrast.
Examples:

Our boss lets us work from home; however, we can’t wear jeans.
Although the economy is growing, prices are rising.

What to Practise More
