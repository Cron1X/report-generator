Encounter 40 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on travel and vacations, polite questions, present perfect with since, and asking for information in a more polite way.

What You Practised

You worked on vacation vocabulary.
Examples you practised:

Where to go
Where to stay
How to travel
What to do

You practised talking about travel plans.
Examples:

I really want to go to Hawaii.
I will stay in a hotel.
I will travel by train.

You practised polite indirect questions.
Examples:

Can you tell me where the hotel is?
Can you tell me what time breakfast is?
Can you tell me which floor my room is on?

You worked with the present perfect using since.
Examples:

I haven’t been on vacation since last summer.
I’ve lived here since 2018.

You practised asking about duration.
Examples:

How long have you lived in this city?
How long have you been a student?

What to Practise More

1. Polite Question Word Order

Can you tell me where the restaurant is?

2. Using since for Time

I’ve known him since 2015.

Final Comment

This unit focused on travel language and polite questions. To improve further, practise indirect questions and present perfect sentences with since.
