Encounter 48 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on health, stress, workplace problems, advice, and talking about unfinished actions using the present perfect. The unit also developed time clauses and describing habits that cause irritation.

What You Practised

You worked on health and stress vocabulary.
Examples you practised:

tired
stressed
ill
sore throat

My head hurts.
I feel stressed and tired.

You practised giving advice.
Examples:

You should sleep more.
You should listen to the doctor.
You need to rest.

You worked on describing annoying habits using always.
Examples:

He’s always yelling at everybody.
He’s always stealing other people’s ideas.

You practised the present perfect with yet and still.
Examples:

I haven’t sent the email yet.
I still haven’t sent the email.

You practised time clauses with as soon as and after.
Examples:

I’ll send the email as soon as I read the notes.
I’ll call you after I finish lunch.

You practised asking about health and stress.
Examples:

What’s wrong with you?
Are you sleeping well?
Why are you so stressed?

What to Practise More

Final Comment

This unit focused on stress, advice, and unfinished actions. To improve further, practise present perfect forms and clear advice language in full sentences.
