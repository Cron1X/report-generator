Encounter 3 – Student Feedback Report

In this unit, we focused on introducing yourself more confidently, talking about times of day, spelling names and addresses, using this and these correctly, and asking and answering basic personal questions in short conversations. You participated well and showed steady improvement using the target language.

You practiced greetings for different times of day and used them appropriately in context.
Examples:
- Good morning.
- Good afternoon.
- Good evening.
- Good night.

You worked on asking for and giving names and spelling them clearly.
Examples:
- What’s your name?
- My name is Emily.
- How do you spell it?
- E-M-I-L-Y.
- Can you spell your last name, please?

Spelling addresses was also an important focus. You practiced listening carefully and spelling words and numbers accurately.
Examples:
- What’s your address?
- My address is 205 West Pine Street.
- Can you spell “Pine”?
- P-I-N-E.

You practiced using this and these to talk about objects around you.
Examples:
- This is a chair.
- This is my bag.
- These are chairs.
- These are my keys.

Countries and nationalities were introduced and practiced in simple sentences.
Examples:
- I’m from China. I’m Chinese.
- She’s from Canada. She’s Canadian.
- They’re from France. They’re French.

During the communication activities, you practiced short conversations that included several questions, such as name, country, and address. You were generally able to follow the conversation pattern and respond appropriately, although at times you needed a little support to keep the conversation moving.

To continue improving, focus on:
- Using full sentences when answering questions
- Spelling names and addresses smoothly without long pauses
- Choosing this or these more quickly
- Keeping eye contact and responding confidently in conversations

Overall, this unit helped you build confidence with introductions and basic personal information. With continued practice, your fluency and accuracy in everyday conversations will continue to improve.
