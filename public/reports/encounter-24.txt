Encounter 24 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on places in town, directions, and talking about past and future actions using going to and the past simple.

What You Practised

You worked with places in town.
Examples you practised:

apartment
house
library
store
museum
market

You practised giving directions.
Examples:

Go out the front door.
Turn left at the first street.
Go straight ahead.

You practised talking about future plans using going to.
Examples:

I’m going to go to the museum.
Are you going to go to the supermarket?

You also practised talking about the past using the past simple.
Examples:

I went to the museum yesterday.
Did you go to the game last week?
What did you do yesterday?

You practised using time expressions.
Examples:

last week
yesterday
next week
tomorrow

What to Practise More

1. Choosing Past or Future Forms

I went to the museum yesterday.
I’m going to go to the museum tomorrow.

2. Giving Directions Clearly

Turn right at the corner.
Walk to the library from there.

Final Comment

This unit focused on directions and time-based language. To improve further, practise choosing the correct tense and giving directions using full sentences.
