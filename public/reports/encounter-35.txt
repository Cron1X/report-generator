Encounter 35 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on travel information, giving recommendations, using should, need to, and could, and talking about availability and future arrangements.

What You Practised

You worked with tourist and travel information.
Examples you practised:

tour information
prices
places to see
transportation

What kind of tours are there?
How much does it cost?

You practised making recommendations.
Examples:

I recommend going to the Empire State Building.
You should visit the museum.

You worked with advice and possibility.
Examples:

You should stay on the right side of the road.
You need to know the speed limit.
I could rent a car.

You practised using there is / there isn’t with anything and something.
Examples:

There’s nothing to do.
Is there anything to do in this town?
There is something to do.

You practised talking about future arrangements.
Examples:

What are you doing this evening?
I’m meeting a friend later tonight.

You practised asking about availability.
Examples:

Are you available this Friday?
Do you have anything to do on Saturday?
