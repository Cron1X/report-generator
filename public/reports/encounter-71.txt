Encounter 71 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on certainty, storytelling, reported speech, and reflecting on past experiences. The unit developed discussion and interpretation skills.

What You Practised

You worked on expressing certainty.
Examples practised:

I think she was a good student.
She must have been successful.
It’s possible she struggled at first.

You practised reported speech.
Examples:

Khae said she couldn’t believe it.
Marco said he was proud.

You worked on past contrasts.
Examples:

In the past, they would spend more time together.
Before, they wouldn’t eat out very often.

You practised sharing personal stories.
Examples:

A time someone helped you solve a problem.
Something funny that happened at school.

You practised predicting outcomes.
Examples:

Their relationship might not be the same.
Things could change very quickly.

What to Practise More

Certainty Expressions
Practise choosing the correct level of certainty.

Narrative Flow
Link ideas smoothly when telling stories.

Final Comment

This unit focused on reflection and storytelling. To improve further, practise structuring stories clearly and using certainty language accurately.
