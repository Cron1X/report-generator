Encounter 17 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on talking about future plans using going to.

What You Practised

You practised forming future plans.
Examples:

I’m going to study tonight.
She’s going to visit her family.
They’re going to travel next week.

You practised asking about future plans.
Examples:

What are you going to do tonight?
Are you going to work tomorrow?

You also practised giving reasons.
Examples:

I’m going to study because I have an exam.
She’s going to rest because she’s tired.

What to Practise More

1. Using going to in Full Sentences

I’m going to meet my friend this evening.

2. Adding Reasons

I’m going to stay at home because I’m tired.

Final Comment

This unit focused on expressing future plans clearly. To improve further, practise using going to confidently and adding reasons to your answers.
