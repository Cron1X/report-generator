Encounter 72 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on describing landscapes, expressing preferences, using descriptive adjectives, and writing polite apologies. The unit developed descriptive and formal communication skills.

What You Practised

You worked on describing landscapes.
Examples practised:

It’s a region with high mountains.
The scenery is very beautiful in winter.
You can find diverse wildlife there.

You practised descriptive vocabulary.
Examples:

conservation
wildlife
diverse
beautiful scenery

You worked on polite workplace language.
Examples:

I must inform you that…
I do apologize for any inconvenience.
If this is acceptable, please let me know.

You practised preference language.
Examples:

I prefer going to the countryside.
Cities are more exciting, but noisier.

You practised giving opinions about places.
Examples:

A canyon is a beautiful sight to see.
A moose is very powerful.

What to Practise More

Descriptive Detail
Add more adjectives when describing places.

Formal Writing
Practise polite and professional tone in messages.
