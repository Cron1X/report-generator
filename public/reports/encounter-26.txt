Encounter 26 – Student Feedback Report

In this lesson, we focused on expressing likes and dislikes, feelings, past events, and opinions using think and because. The aim was to describe experiences and reactions clearly.

What You Practised

You practised expressing likes and dislikes.
Examples:

I like cooking.
I don’t like the new menu.
I can’t stand the new menu.
I really enjoy cooking.

You practised talking about feelings.
Examples:

I feel happy.
I feel excited.
I feel bored.
I feel sad.

You practised talking about past events.
Examples:

What did you do?
Where were you?
What happened?

You practised giving opinions and reasons.
Examples:

I think he is happy because he is smiling.
I like Shen because he is caring.

What to Practise More

1. Explaining Feelings with Reasons

I feel excited because I’m going on vacation.

2. Talking About Past Events Clearly

I felt nervous because I was waiting.

Final Comment

This unit focused on expressing feelings, opinions, and past experiences. To improve further, practise adding reasons with because and using full sentences when describing events.
